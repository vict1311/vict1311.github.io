Exploring Hybrid Digital Boardgames as Multi-Player Design Fiction Probes into Energy Communities

This is the preliminary title of my Master's thesis, which I am writing with my friend Emmelie Christensen. In this thesis, we are designing a hybrid digital board game in which players can engage in the many conflicts and dilemmas of energy communities. We are using the Research through Design method by meticulously documenting our design choices throughout the process to gain knowledge of what we design and how. Further, we will conduct a number of play sessions, which can serve as possibilities to probe players' perspectives on what is fair in energy communities. In doing this, we hope to:

  • Provide insights on how players experience the many dilemmas in energy commnunities, what they deem appropriate, and thus be able to provide design insights relevant for energy communities and their digital energy platforms.
  • Generate knowledge on how we can use the hybrid digital boardgame format as a design fiction probe tool, which can support multiple simultaneous participants, and thus be utilized to probe commons initiatives like energy communities.
